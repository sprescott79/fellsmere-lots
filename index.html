Understanding the Zoning

A-2 Zoning: Residential & Agricultural

These lots are zoned A-2 (Agriculture-2) by Indian River County. A-2 is designed to support both single-family residential use and a range of agricultural uses — making it a flexible designation for buyers who want to live on their land and do something with it.

🏡 Residential

A-2 supports single-family residential development. You can build a home, add accessory structures, and live on your land. It's a true residential parcel — just with the added flexibility of agricultural zoning.

🌾 Agricultural

A-2 is an agricultural zoning district, meaning the land is intended to support farming, ranching, and related uses alongside residential development. Specific uses depend on your parcel and plans.

📞 Have Specific Use Questions? Contact Indian River County Directly.

Zoning regulations are detailed and parcel-specific. If you have questions about what's allowed on a particular lot — building permits, agricultural operations, accessory structures, or anything else — the county's Community Development Department is the right place to start.
